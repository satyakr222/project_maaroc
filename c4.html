FIDIC Contracts Management and Administration

This advanced training course explains and illustrates the practical use of the FIDIC Conditions of Contract for Construction (the “Construction Contract”), the FIDIC Conditions of Contract for Plant and Design-Build (the “Design-Build Contract”) and FIDIC Conditions of Contract EPC/ Turnkey Projects (the “EPC Contract”). The course is designed to further help the participants have confidence in working with these documents whether representing Employers, Consultants or Contractors. It provides extensive checking measures and work exercises to give all delegates a comprehensive understanding of proper management of these contracts.
