FIDIC Design, Build and Operate Contract (DBO Contract) Special Module

This practical training course, provided by Malith Mendis, FIDIC accredited Trainer, discusses the History of FIDIC and its suite of Contracts, explains and illustrates the use of the FIDIC Conditions of Contract for Design, Build and Operate Contract 2008 (DBO Contract).

It is suitable for all the Parties involved with FIDIC DBO Contract – the Employer, the Project Manager, the Contractor, the Engineer, and the DAB Members. By having a good understanding of the FIDIC risk allocation policy and the resulting responsibilities and rights of the Parties under a FIDIC Contract, a more efficient management of the project may be achieved. The Trainer is an Engineer and have had extensive worldwide involvement with management of projects and related dispute resolution.
