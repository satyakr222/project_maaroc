Management of Claims and the Resolution of Disputes under FIDIC 2017 Contracts Reprinted 2022 with Amendments, and training including Case Studies and Mock Adjudication

This practical training course, provided by Malith Mendis, FIDIC accredited Trainer, is designed to provide practical advice to those working with the FIDIC Conditions of Contract in the management of claims, understanding, defending and achieving the resolution to claims. Includes Delay Analysis, Analysis for Extra Payment and introduction to Delay and Disruption protocol and the workings of the Dispute Adjudication Board and Arbitration. Also includes Case Studies and Mock Adjudication.
